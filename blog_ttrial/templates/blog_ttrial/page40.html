The Hero Section

Let's begin by taking a look at the Hero section.

From our previous experience, we can clearly see that the hero section consist of a parent container [let's call it hero-container] housing two child containers [let's call them left-hero and right-hero].

The left-hero contains an image [<img>] while the right-hero contains a heading [<h1>], a descriptive paragraph [<p>] and a link that says 'Explore Featured Products' [<a>].
