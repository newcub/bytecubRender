A Responsive Layout

The Hero & Category Sections

If you have noticed, resizing our beauty page to fit smaller viewports/screens causes the Hero and Category Sections of our page to shrink together until they finally break, making our page less visually appealing on smaller screens. This happens mainly because we used the flex-box to design those sections. Therefore we need to use a @media query to make the flex items collapse [or stack above each other] on smaller screens or viewports.

Beauty Products & Beauty Tips Sections

Also, going beyond 350px causes the page to become even messier due to the fact that the minimum width of the tip-grid items was defined at 350px [grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fit, minmax(350px, 1fr));].

To fix this, we would have to stack the grid items below each other at a viewport width greater than 350px. Let's just go ahead an implement this idea for a better understanding.
