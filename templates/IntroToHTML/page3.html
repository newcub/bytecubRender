2.2 What are HTML Attributes?

Attributes add additional information to a tag. They are usually placed in the opening tag and holds great significance to the information wrapped in the tag.

As seen above, attributes consist of an Attribute Name and an Attribute Value.

The attribute names tells the browser what type of attribute is being called and the attribute value specifies the property of that attribute to be implimented.

The above lang attribute specifies the language used in the paragraph['en' is the short for English].
