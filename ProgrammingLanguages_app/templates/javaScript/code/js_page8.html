Comparison Operators

To make decisions in your conditional statements, you often use comparison operators:

  • > [greater than]
  • < [less than]
  • >= [greater than or equal to]
  • <= [less than or equal to]
  • == [equal to]
  • === [strictly equal to]
  • != [not equal to]
  • !== [strictly not equal to]
